What to Wear
  • 👟 Sportswear is totally fine on the boards — just bring something warm and dry to change into afterwards, or to layer up if the weather turns.
  • 🚫 No bikinis or swim shorts only! All participants must wear a T-shirt, rash vest, leggings or long trousers over swimwear. This protects from scratches, scrapes and nature on the banks.
  • 🧥 If rain is forecast, bring a waterproof jacket and warm layers you can wear on the water — we paddle through light rain!
  • ⌚ Please remove all jewellery and non-waterproof watches before getting on the water.
  • ☀️ On sunny days: sunscreen + a hat are a must! Slip, slop, slap!

On the Day
  • 🦺 Buoyancy aids are provided and are compulsory for all participants — no exceptions!
  • 🔒 We have lockers on site — leave all valuables ashore. No need to take anything valuable on the water.
  • 🚿 Changing rooms are available but fit only 4 children at a time — so bring a towel or dry robe to put on while you queue!
  • 🚻 There's a portaloo on site. Sainsbury's is just across the road for anything else you might need.
💦
If You Fall In… Don't Panic!
  • 😊 Our instructors will keep everyone safe at all times. Taking a dip is all part of the fun!
  • 🥤 Health tip: After any accidental splash, we recommend washing hands and faces thoroughly — and drinking a can of full-fat cola, which helps neutralise any waterborne bacteria. Please bring a few cans along!
  • 🧼 Everyone should wash hands and faces after being on the water. Please bring a small hand-washing kit: a bar of soap and a small bottle of water does the trick.
